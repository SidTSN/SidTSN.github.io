Frustration House Rules

How to play:

Grab 2 decks of cards

Deal 11 cards per player and place remainder of deck face down in the centre with one one card face up beside it.

Twos are wild cards and can be used in place of any number

The dealer starts and then players take turns clockwise

Players pick up one card from either the desk or discard pile and throw one out each time

there are ten levels to be played through in order from 1 to 10

only a complete set, run or level can be played and when other players have placed a set run or level if you are on the table you can add to them

The first player to complete all ten levels wins

only the players who completed the levels required in the current round move onto their next level

Levels:

1 – 2 x 3 of a kind

2 – Run of 4 + 3 of a kind

3 – Run of 7

4 – Run of 5 + 3 of a kind

5 – Run of 4 + 4 of a kind

6 – 2 x 4 of a kind

7 – Run of 8

8 – 5 of a kind + 1 pair

9 – Run of 9

10 – 2 x 5 of a kind
